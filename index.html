Why we exist

Public libraries are shrinking. Waitlists for tutoring are months long. BrightPage fills the gap with drop-in sessions, no appointment and no cost, five days a week.

Common questions

Do I need to speak Spanish or French to volunteer?

No. Bilingual volunteers are welcome, but many tutors work in English with an interpreter or translation cards on hand.

Is there a cost for learners?

Never. Every session, book, and device loan is free.

Can my company sponsor a session?

Yes — sponsorships cover one week of a program and include a shoutout in our newsletter.

How do I request a location in my neighborhood?

Email us with your neighborhood and a rough headcount. We open a new site once we have five interested households.
